# Ecommerce_analysis_dashboard-Power-BI-
📊 README – Ecommerce Sales Dashboard
📌 Project Title

Ecommerce Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

# 📖 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of ecommerce sales data using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into revenue performance, customer behaviour, product trends, and regional sales distribution to support data-driven business decisions.

The goal of this project is to identify sales growth patterns, top-performing products, and improvement opportunities across different segments.

# 🎯 Objectives

Analyse overall sales performance

- Identify top-performing products and categories

- Track profit and revenue trends over time

- Understand customer purchasing behaviour

- Compare regional performance

- 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

- Power BI (Data Modelling & Visualization)

- DAX (KPIs & Calculated Measures)

- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

- Data Modelling (Relationships, Star Schema if used)

# 📊 Dashboard Features

- Revenue & Profit Overview

- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

- Category-wise & Product-wise Performance

- Customer Segmentation

- Regional Sales Comparison

- Interactive Filters & Slicers

# 📈 Key Insights (Example Outcomes)

- Sales growth trend across months

- Top revenue-generating product category

- Region contributing highest profit

- Repeat vs new customer behaviour

- Profit margin variation across products

# 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Based on the Ecommerce Sales Dashboard analysis, the following KPIs were used to evaluate business performance:

-  1️⃣ Total Sales

      Represents the overall revenue generated from all ecommerce transactions displayed in the dashboard. This KPI provides a high-level view of business performance.

-  2️⃣ Total Profit

      Indicates the net profit earned after deducting costs. It helps assess the financial health and sustainability of the business.

-  3️⃣ Profit Margin (%)

     Calculated as (Total Profit / Total Sales) × 100.
     This KPI measures operational efficiency and pricing effectiveness.

-  4️⃣ Total Orders

     Shows the total number of transactions completed. It helps understand sales volume and customer activity level.

-  5️⃣ Quantity Sold

     Displays the total number of units sold across all categories. Useful for inventory and demand analysis.

-  6️⃣ Sales by Category

      Highlights which product categories contribute the most to overall revenue. This supports product strategy and marketing focus.

-  7️⃣ Sales by Region

      Shows geographical performance distribution, helping identify high-performing and underperforming regions.

-  8️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend

Tracks revenue performance over time to identify growth patterns, seasonality, and fluctuations.
